About Us

We are a cleaning company that offers cleaning services throughout Norway. We operate mostly in Oslo and the surrounding area. We have regularly helped you maintain cleanliness and order. We guarantee accuracy, solidity, regularity, short waiting time and competitive prices. Every customer is equally important to us. We treat each customer individually. With us, we place great emphasis on high quality of our services. Our employees have been thoroughly trained. Thanks to this, we can ensure the highest quality. We clean private houses, apartments, cabins, offices, business premises, stairs, kindergartens, etc.
We offer regular cleaning services, mostly every week or every other week, to private customers and companies. Our company offers cleaning in newly renovated objects, before viewings, before and after the sale of a home. The increasing number of satisfied customers is proof of a job well done and motivates us to constant development. We are open to all suggestions and constructive comments. We carefully follow all instructions on the use of cleaning equipment. We follow exactly the way and the order of services performed. We have a great deal of experience gathered through many years of practice. We fulfill all customers' wishes regarding cleaning. Detailed information about our company can be found on our website.
